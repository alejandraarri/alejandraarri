About me

Hi! I’m Alejandra Arri and I’m a Telecommunications Engineer who has been working as a Full Stack Java developer for 4 years. I am a geeky girl who tells nerd (and bad) jokes all the time. I also love Web Design. I’m creative, perfectionist and meticulous; the little details are what bring the whole design together.

I used to think that being a good professional meant leaving aside my creative mind and focussing on analytic stuff but over time I’ve realised I’m happier when I can use both points of view. And I’ve found a way to get the most of my coder techy side and my creative side: I’d like to focus on Front-End. It allows me to create, to code (and get the satisfaction when something you were stuck on finally starts working) but also allows me to express myself in a creative way. That’s why it's what I love most.
